Teradata Branding follow these usages of the new logo, font & colors
Components of the logo

Modern, bold and conveying stability, the new Teradata logo consists of two components: a wordmark, followed by a period. The wordmark incorporates customized typographic elements implying connectivity and flow. The period is an essential component of the mark that speaks to the definitive and conclusive nature of the Teradata brand.

Teradata Typeface
Primary Typeface: Ridley Grotesk

The new Teradata brand typeface is Ridley Grotesk, an open, approachable, modern sans serif with a full range of weights.

Ridley Grotesk should be used in all design materials, marketing communications, external advertising and on the web.

Teradata Color Palettes
Core palette

For the brand redesign, we’ve evolved the core colors to create a palette that is vibrant and modern, without sacrificing stability. Colors may be screened for added tonal variation. Tints shown are at 80%, 60%, 40% and 20%, but others may be used where appropriate.

slate
orange
Supplemental palette

In addition to evolving the core colors, we’ve added a supplemental color palette for added flexibility in specialized design situations.

Supplemental colors should be used sparingly and only when necessary. For example, certain UI (user interfaces) with a need for multiple distinguishing colors, or specific campaign materials.

yellow
teal
blue
 UI Color Schemes

Console Color Scheme

Core consoles & products (such as IntelliCloud Management Console, Control Center, Server Management) color palette is Slate & Orange:

Consoles Dashboard label Primary: Teradata Slate (700 hue) label Accent: Teradata Orange (800 hue) label Warn: Material Design Deep Orange (800 hue)

Apps & Field Solution Color Scheme

Non-core products & field solutions (such as AppCenter apps, Customer Journey & CIM/RTIM) color palette is Slate & Teal:

Apps & Solutions Dashboard label Primary: Teradata Slate (700 hue) label Accent: Teradata Teal (800 hue) label Warn: Material Design Deep Orange (800 hue)

Analytics & Monitoring Color Scheme

Analytics & Monitoring products (such as Viewpoint, Unity System Monitoring & Workload Analytics) color palette is Slate & Teal:

Analytics & Monitoring Dashboard label Primary: Teradata Slate (700 hue) label Accent: Teradata Blue (800 hue) label Warn: Material Design Deep Orange (800 hue)
